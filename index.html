01 The Goal

A Clear Path
Forward

The goal of this roadmap is simple: to build a clear, honest path where your role, responsibility, leadership, and earning potential can grow as Daily Grind Media grows. This isn't a fixed ceiling or a rigid contract — it's a shared direction we move toward together.

As you take on more, deliver stronger results, and help drive the business forward, your opportunity grows with it. The more impact you create, the more value you build — and the more room there is for that value to be recognized.

Increased responsibility, stronger leadership, and business growth create increased earning potential — reviewed together as the business evolves.

This Goes
Both Ways

This roadmap isn't just about your growth — it's about my responsibility as the founder to keep building Daily Grind Media into a company worth growing inside. A path only means something if there's a real business behind it, and that part is on me.

So here's what I'm committing to. As you keep raising your game, I'll keep doing the work to make sure there's more room, more opportunity, and a bigger vision for both of us to build toward.

Continue bringing in new clients. More work coming in is what makes everything else possible.

Continue improving our systems. Better systems make great work repeatable and your job easier.

Invest back into the business. Reinvesting in tools, people, and capacity so we can grow.

Build a team around us. Surrounding you with a bench of talent so no one carries it alone.

Create opportunities for leadership. Real roles to grow into as the company expands.

Build a company where great people build careers. Somewhere worth staying and growing for the long term.
